EXPERIMENTS

  • PURPOSE: to observe gluten at work and understand the chemistry of bread dough!
  • PROCEDURE: followed instructions outlined by Margaret Hill in her article Attack of the Gluten in ChemMatters.
  • HYPOTHESIS: the dough will increase in size.
  • DATA:
    # description time mass (g)
    1 pre-proving 1:29:31 PM 575 g
    2 post-proving 4:35:46 PM 545 g
  • OBSERVATIONS:
    • the dough was quite sticky at first, which made the kneading process very difficult.
    • after ~3 hours, the dough increased greatly in size, by a factor of about 3. (while it only took up about 1/2 the bowl initially, after several hours it began to rise over the edges of the bowl.)
    • the mass of the dough decreased by 30 g in 3 hours
